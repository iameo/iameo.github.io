No? not present?! bummer. Ok, we'll have to use a country close to Nigeria in terms of Population, and/or GDP, and/or "third worldiness" Alright?

According to nationonline, there's a bit of semblance with Zimbabwe(which is listed in the data) in terms of Human Development Index (HDI).

HDI: A composite index measuring average achievement in three basic dimensions of human development-a long and healthy life, knowledge and a decent standard of living. Isn't that a good metrics for possible suicide? so Zimbabwe.

But first, let's do a bit of wide research before jumping to a country close to Nigeria. I would like to know some more details; country with the highest number of suicides, the lowest, the age bracket with the most suicides, and why isn't Nigeria included?!!
